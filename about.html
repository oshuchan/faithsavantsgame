ABOUT THE GAME

FAITH Savants: Gamified Learning
100+ TRIAL RUNS . 40+ TESTERS
First and foremost, the game's specifications call for minimal data consumption and storage space consumption, equivalent to that of lite versions of software. Another feature is spatialized voice chat, which allows students to hear other players in the game based on their location in the room and proximity to the user. The next one is to interact within a 2D room with students or teachers. Using the share-screen or app-share feature, students and teachers can interact/communicate in-game.
Users, on the other hand, have complete freedom to choose from numerous kinds of maps/rooms available in the game, as well as create their own public or private rooms for their classes. To avoid in-game skin issues and such, the students and teachers have default skins for male and female users.
Faith Savants: Gamified Learning is a simple game with a straightfoward interface which comprises aspect of the design such as pixelized sprites, beginner-friendly game user-interface and vibrant colors.
