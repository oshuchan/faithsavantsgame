The main plot line starts out bright and cheery, but as we discover more about how the individuals may have taken their contact learning and overall social interaction for granted, the story takes on a deeper meaning. They hold discussions with one another about the problems of learning at home, possibly tumultuous home environments, or the inability to be alone.
